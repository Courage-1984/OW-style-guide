# Open Window Website Content & Architecture Audit

**Source:** www.openwindow.co.za & ow.uxidevelopment.co.za
**Date Compiled:** March 2026

## 1. Brand Identity & Positioning

* **Tagline/Main Heading:** Educational Leader in Creative Arts & Technologies.
* **Sub-headline:** Private Higher Education offering Postgraduate, Degree and Higher Certificate qualifications in Creative Fields.
* **Brand Introduction:** Welcome to Open Window, South Africa’s leading institute for creative and digital arts. Established in 1993, Open Window has grown from its origins as a specialist art school into a respected private higher education institution focused on visual communication, film arts, animation, and interaction design.
* **Vision:** It is the vision of Open Window to be a leader and trendsetter in the education sector of South Africa and internationally by achieving product excellence through academic rigour and industry relevance, the use of cutting-edge technology and the continuous upgrading of infrastructure in a customer-centric environment.
* **Mission:** To deliver creative and critical thinkers who possess the technical, dynamic, professional and multi-skilled capabilities globally required by the creative industries. Achieved through superior educational models, cutting-edge technology, and international competitive outcomes.
* **Teaching Methodology:** "Creative Hybrid" – A dynamic multimodal teaching approach blending virtual engagement with on-campus experiences (Campus-First, Multimodal Rotation, and Virtual-First).

## 2. Global Navigation Structure (Information Architecture)

The primary navigation hierarchy is structured to funnel prospective students, current students, and faculty.

* **Home**
* **About Us:** Alumni Success Stories, Graduate Portfolios, Awards, Faculty, Campus Life.
* **Qualifications (The Core Product):**
* *Postgraduate:* Honours in Visual Communication, Postgraduate Diploma in Creative Practice.
* *Degree:* Visual Communication Design, Film Arts, Creative Technologies, Game Design & Animation.
* *Higher Certificate:* 3D Animation, Digital Film Production, Graphic Design, Photography & Videography, User Interface Design.


* **Campus Life:** Centurion Campus, Stellenbosch Campus, Blairgowrie Creative Hub, Events, Clubs.
* **Admissions:** Apply Now, Enquire, Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL).
* **Contact Us**
* **Resources:** Academic & Learning, Fees & Financial, Campus Guide, International & General Docs.
* **Utility/Top Bar Links:** Community Dashboard, Apply Now, Academic Calendar.

## 3. Key Content Pillars & Department Definitions

The site relies heavily on visually categorizing the four main academic departments. NotebookLLM should understand these distinctions for UI design:

* **Visual Communication:** "A progressive platform for learners to explore Communication Design, Illustration, and Photography."
* **Film Arts:** "Audio-visual communication, with skills that enable students to work in tomorrow's demanding time-based media environment."
* **Animation Arts:** "Film Arts degree with a choice of three major subjects such as 3D Animation, Game Design and Motion Design."
* **Creative Technologies:** "Innovative study in the diverse and evolving fields of Interactive Development, User Experience Design, and Industrial Design."

## 4. Copywriting & Tone of Voice Observations

* **Tone:** Forward-looking, academic yet highly practical, professional, innovative, and premium.
* **Formatting:** Capitalized main structural call-to-actions (e.g., "EXPLORE", "APPLY NOW", "ENQUIRE").
* **Microcopy:** Section headers are direct ("Explore Our Qualifications", "How to Apply", "Our Campuses", "Industry-Leading Lecturers", "Curriculum Built for the Real World").
* **Naming Conventions:** Focus on "Creative Hybrid", "MakerSpace", "NOW Gallery", emphasizing physical and digital creation.

## 5. UX & UI Functional Requirements (Inferred from Staging)

* **Typography Sourcing:** The staging site pulls Google Fonts: `Montserrat` (Primary/Headers), `Quicksand` (Accents/Subheaders), and `DM Sans` (Body text/Legibility). (Cross-reference this with your existing *Headings and Font Weights* text file).
* **Card/Grid Elements:** The site utilizes distinct content cards for "The Creatives" (e.g., Student Spotlights like Lara Reid, Jade Rawson) and "Events" (Stardust on Broken Ground, Riso Roulette, Figure Friday Remix).
* **Media Integration:** The design relies heavily on video background capabilities, high-resolution SVG logos, and gallery grids for student portfolio showcases.
